⚖️ Digital Courts Rules amp; Conduct

Required Agreement for Virtual Court Access

⚠️ IMPORTANT: By accessing VideoCourts™, you agree to comply with all court rules. Violations may result in removal and sanctions.

1. General Conduct

  • Dress appropriately (business attire)
  • Address judge as "Your Honor"
  • Remain muted when not speaking
  • Camera must stay ON during proceedings
  • No recording or screenshotting

2. Technical Requirements

  • Stable internet (5+ Mbps)
  • Quiet, well-lit private location
  • Test setup 24hrs before hearing
  • Join 10-15 minutes early

3. Biometric Verification

  • Complete enrollment before first appearance
  • Identity verification required each session
  • Biometric data encrypted, used for auth only
