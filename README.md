# VideoCourts™ — Institutional Justice Technology Stack

Sovereign Virtual Proceedings • Identity Integrity • Evidence Continuity

## Overview
VideoCourts™ is a court-aligned, non-adjudicative technology platform designed to support remote and hybrid judicial proceedings through secure access control, identity verification, evidence integrity, and operational orchestration.

The system is intentionally non-invasive, non-decisional, and deployment-flexible, allowing courts to pilot modern virtual proceedings without rip-and-replace, without vendor lock-in, and without delegating legal judgment to software.

VideoCourts is built and stewarded by a single founder with formal legal training (paralegal, non-lawyer), technical systems experience, and multi-year continuity in justice-focused infrastructure development.
